Who We Are

Mosbots is the official robotics team of Moscrop Secondary School in Burnaby, BC, competing in the VEX V5 Robotics Competition (V5RC) under team number 32987A. We're a group of students united by a shared passion for engineering, problem-solving, and building robots that can hold their own on the competition field.

Every season, we design, build, program, and drive a new robot from the ground up to compete in that year's V5RC game. From brainstorming mechanisms to writing autonomous routines, every part of the process is student-led — giving us hands-on experience in robotics, coding, and teamwork that goes way beyond the classroom. Whether you're a seasoned competitor or just curious about what we do, we're always happy to share our love of robotics. Follow along as we take on new challenges, refine our builds, and represent Moscrop on the competition field
